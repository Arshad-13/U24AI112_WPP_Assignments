SVNIT Team Wins Smart India Hackathon

Team Agrani from SVNIT Surat has achieved a remarkable milestone by winning the Grand Finale of the Smart India Hackathon 2024, competing among 50,000 teams nationwide. Under the guidance of mentors Dr. Balu Parne and Dr. Chandra Prakash, they developed an innovative solution for the Government of NCT Delhi to automate fire department processes, earning a cash prize of ₹1,00,000, representing SVNIT on the National Level.
